Overview

We sucessfully used Business Intelligence principles to extract data from our csv file and perform the necessary transformations to load data into our data warehouse. We performed OLAP operations such as slicing, dicing, pivot, roll-up and drill-down to find ptterns between different attributes. We were also able to predict classes and values using ML algorithms like classification and regression and group similar restaurants together using clustering. Various visualizations were used to help interpret the relationship between data attributes. We also generated a complete report and customized reports compiling all the operations performed.
